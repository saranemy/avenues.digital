About

My name is Sara, and I'm a UI/UX designer. Before discovering my passion for web design, I worked as a print designer specializing in advertising and helped many clients reach their marketing goals. Then I discovered the world of UI and UX, and realized there are many similarities between UI and graphic design when it comes to user-centered problem-solving. However, unlike print design, web design allowed me to connect my love for design and modern technologies.

I was able to transfer my skills in analyzing possible customer/end-user needs, as well as knowledge in graphic design, to create intuitive and accessible platforms.

HTML, CSS, JS, PHP

Designed and implemented web sites using HTML5, CSS and JavaScript.

Graphic Design

Using effective typography, color theory and other graphic design principles to create visual hierarchy.

User-Oriented

Designing effective platforms involves thinking of the end-user, and understanding what they may percieve as intuitive or logical, at any computer literacy level.

Accessibility

It is important to think of possible physical challenges in human-computer interaction - disorders, hand and finger sizes, language barriers, and others.
